### Policy 0.01: Department Lead Prerequisites
- All members who wish to be the head of a Department, or Sub-Department must be part of our Staff Team, to ensure that our community has trusted people within department leadership.

### Policy 0.02: Staff Team Subrole Prerequisites
- All members that wish to be Support or Partnership Staff must already be part of our Staff Team.

### Policy 0.03: Un-Whitelisted Department Roleplay
- All un-whitelisted members will be allowed to join a LEO team once without the need to apply, if they wish to join anytime after that, they will have to apply.
